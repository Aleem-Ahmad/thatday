💌 A Letter From My Heart To Yours

My dearest, most incredible, most irreplaceable Faimah,

Well, today is that day. So grab your coziest blanket, make yourself a cup of something warm, and let me pour every single feeling I have ever felt about you onto these pages. Because you deserve every word, and honestly, fifty thousand words would not be enough — but I will give it everything I have.

Do you remember the first time we actually talked? I do. I remember it the way you remember the first song that ever made you cry — not just the melody but the exact quality of light in the room, the feeling in the air, the way time seemed to slow down and say: pay attention, this one matters.

You walked in, and I do not know how to explain this without sounding dramatic, but you were different. There was this warmth around you that was not just warmth — it was safety. The kind that does not announce itself, the kind that just quietly rearranges the air so that breathing becomes easier. I felt it immediately. I thought: this girl is going to mean something to me. And I was right. You mean everything.

Within hours of knowing you, I had already told you something I had never told anyone. Do you know how rare that is? Most people spend years building walls. You did not even give me time to pick up the bricks. You walked into my life and somehow, effortlessly, all the things I had been guarding just relaxed. That is a gift, Faimah. That is a deeply rare, deeply precious gift — the ability to make someone feel so seen, so accepted, so genuinely welcomed, that their defenses simply dissolve. You have that. You have always had that.

And in that moment, without knowing it, without any ceremony or announcement, we became friends. Not the kind of friends who float in and out of each other's lives. The kind who anchor each other. The kind who become a permanent fixture in each other's story. The forever kind.
